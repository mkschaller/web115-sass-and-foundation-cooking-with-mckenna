Stuffed Peppers

  • Description
    Beef and rice stuffed peppers with brown sugar tomato sauce make a delicious dinner any night of the week. For an easy weeknight dinner, prep this meal the night before and put it in your crock pot. Take your crockpot out in the morning and let cook on low throughout the day.
  • Ingredients
      Peppers:
    • 1 lb. Ground Beef
    • 1 Packet Onion Soup Mix
    • 4 Bell Peppers
    • 1 cup Minute Instant White Rice
    • 1 Egg
    Sauce:
    • 2 cups (large can) Tomato Soup
    • 1/2 cup Brown Sugar
  • Directions
      1. Preheat oven to 350°F.
      2. Cook rice according to manufacturer's instructions and place in freezer to cool quickly.
      3. Cut the tops off of the peppers and get them. If they do not stand, trim the bottoms so they stand up. Chop the tops of the peppers and put into a medium size bowl.
      4. Add thawed ground beef, cooled rice, onion soup mix, and egg to the bowl with the green peppers. Mix with your hands until well incorporated. Divide mixture into four parts and place each quarter of the mixtre into a pepper. Place peppers into a high edged cooking dish, making sure there is some room between each pepper.
      5. In now emptied bowl, place contents of can of tomato soup and the brown sugar and mix. Pour over the peppers in the dish first and then let surround the peppers.
      6. Cover dish with foil and bake in the center of the oven for 45-50 minutes. Uncover and spoon sauce on top of the peppers so they hold some sauce. Bake for an additional 10-15 minutes, be careful to watch so the brown sugar sauce doesn't burn. Check that the peppers are done by cutting one open or using a meat thermometer.
