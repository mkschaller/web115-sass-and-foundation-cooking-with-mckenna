Measuring Brown Sugar

Brown sugar should not be measured the same as other dry ingredients. Unlike flour or other dry ingredients which should be spooned into measuring cups, brown sugar should be packed into measuring cups. To get the correct amount of brown sugar in a recipe, make sure to spoon your brown sugar into your measuring cup and pack it in with the back of a spoon. Once the cup is full, level the brown sugar. It is then the correct amount for your recipe.
