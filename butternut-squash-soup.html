Butternut Squash Soup

  • Description
    Smoothly pulsed butternut squash and onions mixed with a seasoned chicken stock for a healthy, easy fall and winter soup. The best part? It's a slow cooker soup!
  • Ingredients
      Peppers:
    • 1 Butternut Squash, peeled, deseeded, and roughly chopped
    • 1 Medium Yellow Onion, roughly chopped
    • 4 Cloves of Garlic (about 1 T)
    • 1 (32 oz) box of Chicken Stock or Broth (Broth will have more flavor)
    • 1/4 cup of milk (Coconut milk reccomended)
    • Drizzle Olive Oil
    Seasoning:
    • 1/2 t ginger
    • 1 t cumin
    • 1/2 t paprika
    • Dash of Cayenne
    • 1 1/2 t Sea Salt
    • 1/2 t Black Pepper
    • 1 t Thyme
  • Directions
      1. In crock pot, combine squash, onion, and garlic.
      2. Drizzle vegetables with olive oil and add chicken stock/broth. Sprinkle all seasonings except thyme over the top.
      3. Cook on high heat for four hours.
      4. Pulse in a blender or use an immersion blender to pusle the soup until smooth.
      5. Add milk and thyme, and blend a few seconds more.
