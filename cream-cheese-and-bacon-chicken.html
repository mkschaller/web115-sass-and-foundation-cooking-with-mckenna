Cream Cheese and Bacon Chicken

  • Description
    Savory cream cheese with chive and onions makes a flavorful stuffing for a moist chicken with a crisp bacon shell.
  • Ingredients
    • 8 oz Chive and Onion Cream Cheese
    • 4 Medium Chicken Breasts
    • 8 Strips Bacon
  • Directions
      1. Preheat oven to 375°F.
      2. Spray 8x8 baking dish (one with sides).
      3. Butterfly cut your chicken.
      4. Scoop a quarter of the cream cheese with a spoon and stuff the chicken with it. Butterflying the chicken allows for even cream cheese distribution.
      5. Wrapped the stuff piece of chicken with two strips of bacon each so that the edges are sealed. This will keep the cream cheese from leaking out.
      6. Place in dish and bake covered with foil anywhere from 35-45 minutes, depending on your oven. Uncover for the last five minutes for extra crispy bacon.
